Why we created this app?

As a singer, i always found it hard to first record vocals and then record music videos to post it on other social medias and then share it, so we have created this app where you not only can post your covers, but checkout the covers of different people too. Some of the problems i faced were:

Have trouble in editing music videos for your covers.

As a music enthusiast, i always found it hard to record a good music video for my covers and to edit it, so that i can post them on different social medias.

Takes a lot of time.

It takes a lot of time to record a cover and then record a music video and then post it.

I can't post my covers on different Music platforms, it has to be an original.

Music platforms nowadays, have algorithms where u can't post a cover of a song unless u have copyright license, and it sucks for the average singers like us.

My friends and family wants to listen to my covers

My relatives, wants to listen to my covers, but i can't send everyone, and it's so hard to make a music video and then upload it on social platforms.

Can't post Karaoke Covers.

It feels so hard to share just your audio of the song you like, with like 100 people.

I love listening to covers of the song i like

PErsonally, i love to listen to various covers of the song i like, and see where do my cover stands.
